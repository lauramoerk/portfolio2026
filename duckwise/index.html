Remote Specialist Support in Acute Care

Reducing patient transfers through real-time immersive collaborationt
2024

This project explores how spatial computing and Apple Vision Pro can improve communication in acute healthcare situations. In collaboration with Duckwise, we developed a concept that enables specialists at Rigshospitalet to provide real-time remote support to local hospitals through immersive visual guidance, live patient data, and shared decision-making.

The goal was to reduce unnecessary patient transfers, improve treatment speed, and support healthcare professionals in high-pressure situations. Through user research, interviews with clinicians, and prototype testing, we designed an integrated solution that fits into existing hospital workflows and addresses the real challenges of information sharing and collaboration in critical care.
